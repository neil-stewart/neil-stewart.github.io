Erratum for Stewart (2009)

25 Mar 2020 - Neil

The first equation on p. 1051 of Stewart (2009) should read

$1/[1+(1/d-1)^T]$

instead of

$1/[1+(1-1/d)^T]$

The second equation on p. 1051 should read

$\sum_{i=0}^{T-1}\frac{(T+i-1)!}{i!(T-1)!} ~ p_A^T p_b^i$

instead of

$\sum_{i=0}^{T-1}\frac{(T-i-1)!}{i!(T-1)!} ~ p_A^T p_b^i$
