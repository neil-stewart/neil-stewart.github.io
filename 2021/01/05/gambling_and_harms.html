Gambling and associated harms

05 Jan 2021 - Neil

Muggleton et al. (2021) was published yesterday. We quantify the association between gambling and related financial, social, health, and mortality harms using mass bank transaction data from hundreds of thousands people.

You can read our comment in the New Scientist here

Muggleton, N., Parpart, P., Newall, P., Leake, D., Gathergood, J., & Stewart, N. (2021). The association between gambling and financial, social, and health outcomes in big financial data. Nature Human Behaviour. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41562-020-01045-w
